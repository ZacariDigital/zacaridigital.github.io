Plymouth Boat Trip Project

In the module, you will use graphic–, and motion– design techniques to communicate ideas and information persuasively. The tasks require you to research and develop work iteratively, working across paper, planning and various software packages, to produce a promotional piece, submitted electronically, for the Impact Lab. There are two tasks within this module:

1. Make a short promotional piece responding to a live brief from the Impact Lab. This will make use of assets produced in the Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, or within Unity or, if you choose, a combination of these. You should still seek to convey data/information about the scientific benefits of the project.

2. Create a Design Journal including Critical Evaluation, produced in InDesign.
